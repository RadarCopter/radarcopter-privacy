RadarCopter Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 29, 2026

Quick Summary: RadarCopter only collects location data (real-time, not stored) and a device identifier (not linked to your identity). We don't collect emails, names, or any personal information. We don't sell data or share it with advertisers.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how RadarCopter ("we," "us," or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our iOS application. RadarCopter is designed to detect and display police helicopters in real-time within Spanish territory.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

RadarCopter collects your precise location (GPS coordinates) to provide the core functionality of the app:

  • Your location is sent to our servers in real-time
  • Location data is NOT permanently stored on our servers
  • We process your location immediately to detect nearby helicopters and then discard it
  • Location updates occur only when the app is active or running in the background (if you enable background location)

2.2 Device Identifier

We generate a unique device identifier (UUID) that is:

  • Created automatically when you first launch the app
  • Stored securely in your device's iOS Keychain
  • Used to authenticate your device with our servers
  • Used to associate your device with your Apple subscription (managed by Apple)
  • NOT linked to your personal identity, name, email, or phone number

3. Information We DO NOT Collect

RadarCopter does NOT collect:

  • ❌ Email addresses or phone numbers
  • ❌ Names or personal identification
  • ❌ Passwords or account credentials
  • ❌ Photos, contacts, or messages
  • ❌ Browsing history or search history
  • ❌ Data from other apps on your device
  • ❌ Social media profiles or accounts
  • ❌ Payment information (handled by Apple)

4. How We Use Your Information

4.1 Location Data

Your location is used to:

  • Verify you are within Spain (service is only available in Spanish territory)
  • Detect police helicopters near your current position
  • Display helicopter positions on the radar interface
  • Calculate distance and bearing to detected helicopters

4.2 Device Identifier

Your device ID is used to:

  • Authenticate API requests to our servers
  • Prevent abuse and implement rate limiting
  • Verify subscription status with Apple's StoreKit

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT sell your data.

We do NOT share your data with advertisers.

We have NO partnerships with law enforcement.

5.1 Service Providers

Your data is only processed by:

  • Our Servers: Location data is processed in real-time to detect helicopters (not stored permanently)
  • Apple Inc.: Subscription purchases and management are handled entirely by Apple through StoreKit. Apple may collect payment information according to their own privacy policy

5.2 Data We Access

RadarCopter accesses publicly available aviation data from third-party sources (FlightRadar24 SDK) to detect police helicopters. This data includes:

  • Aircraft positions (ADS-B transponder broadcasts)
  • Aircraft registration numbers
  • Callsigns and flight information

This aviation data is public information and is not linked to any user of our app.

6. Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period
Location Data NOT stored (real-time processing only)
Device ID Stored until you delete the app
Subscription Status Managed by Apple (we query on-demand, don't store)

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS/TLS Encryption: All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted
  • Keychain Storage: Device ID is stored in iOS Keychain (hardware-encrypted)
  • API Authentication: Server requests are authenticated with device ID and API secret
  • No Persistent Storage: Location data is never written to disk or databases
  • Rate Limiting: Prevents abuse and unauthorized access

8. Your Rights and Choices

8.1 Location Access Control

You have full control over location access:

  • Grant or deny location permission at any time via iOS Settings → RadarCopter → Location
  • Choose between "While Using the App" or "Always" (for background alerts)
  • The app allows access to settings and subscription management even without location permission
  • Note: Radar features require location to function

8.2 Data Deletion

To delete your data:

  • Delete the app: All data stored on your device (including device ID) is permanently removed
  • Contact us: Email support@radarcopter.org to request deletion of any server-side data associated with your device ID

8.3 Subscription Management

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID:

  • Open iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → RadarCopter
  • Or visit apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions
  • Cancel anytime (access continues until end of billing period)

9. Children's Privacy

RadarCopter is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@radarcopter.org and we will delete it immediately.

10. Geographic Restrictions

RadarCopter is designed for use within Spanish territory only (Spain mainland, Balearic Islands, Canary Islands, Ceuta, and Melilla). The app verifies your location to ensure you are within the service area. Use outside Spain results in limited functionality.

11. International Data Transfers

Our servers are located within the European Union. If you use the app from outside the EU, your location data (processed in real-time only) may be transmitted to our EU-based servers for processing.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • Updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page
  • Notifying users via an in-app message (for significant changes)

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
